About Us

World-Class Learning for Every Child

2K Kiddo International Standard Curriculum Public School is dedicated to providing world-class early childhood education through activity-based learning, international teaching methodologies, caring and qualified teachers, and a nurturing, safe environment. Located in the heart of Athipet, Ambattur, Chennai, we believe every child deserves the best possible start in life.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before taking the next step.

2K Kiddo offers an internationally benchmarked curriculum, experienced and caring teachers, state-of-the-art facilities, CCTV-monitored safety, nutritious meals, and a holistic development approach — all in a warm, child-friendly environment in the heart of Ambattur.
We follow an International Standard Curriculum that blends the best of global early childhood education frameworks with Indian cultural values. Our activity-based approach focuses on cognitive, social, emotional, and physical development.
Yes! Our full-day daycare service is available for working parents. It includes healthy meals, supervised nap time, structured learning activities, and safe, monitored care from morning until evening.
All our teachers hold recognized early childhood education qualifications and undergo regular professional development workshops. They are trained in child psychology, inclusive education, first aid, and our specific curriculum methodology.
We admit children from 1.5 years to 5.5 years across our programs: Play Group (1.5–2.5 yrs), Pre-KG (2.5–3.5 yrs), Junior KG (3.5–4.5 yrs), and Senior KG (4.5–5.5 yrs). Our Activity Centre and After School Programs are open to all age groups.
